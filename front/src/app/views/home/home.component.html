Welcome to the Document Upload System

1. Input Your Notes

Easily add your notes the way you prefer! You can:
1. Type Directly: Jot down your thoughts or paste content right into the editor.
2. Upload Files: Upload documents in formats like PDF, Word (DOCX), or plain text files.
3. Drag and Drop: Simply drag your files into the upload area for quick processing.

2. Students Condense Lecture Notes

Students upload lengthy lecture notes to the AI Note Summarizer. The tool quickly extracts key points and concepts, creating a concise study guide. This enables more efficient review and improved retention of important information.

3. Export and Share

Once your notes are summarized, you can:
1. Export: Download your summary in formats like PDF, PNG, or Text.
2. Share: Instantly get a unique URL to share your notes with others.
3. Save: Keep your notes organized by saving them directly to your device.
